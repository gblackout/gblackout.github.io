About Me

I'm a Machine Learning PhD at Gerogia Tech supervised by Prof. Le Song. I received my MS in Computational Data Science from CMU in 2017 and BEng in Software Engineering from Beihang Univ. in 2016.

I'm interested in interpretable machine learning models and learning methods that can reduce human-supervision. Typical topics and techniques involved in my research are: learning-by-asking, efficient inference on knowledge graph, logic programming and commonsense reasoning.
